Sprint 3 Reflection

Sprint 3 Work

In the final sprint, my primary focus was finalizing the front-end implementation within the Lovable platform to bridge the gap between our Figma designs and the live prototype. I concentrated on refining the visual hierarchy of the Account Creation and Booking interfaces, and spent much effort debugging the user flow. While visually complete, account creation lacked backend persistence due to a low-code environment without a database.

I mapped JSON files of destinations, flight details, and promotions to UI components—allowing the prototype to display routes & pricing and demonstrate the user journey visually to stakeholders. I also added the special deals carousel, upgraded the flight & check-in flow, linked the logo to home, and included real Avelo terms/contact data.

Overall Learning

My biggest takeaway: design and engineering are not the same. Figma designs need adaptation for real prototypes—this project honed my technical trade-off decisions and cross-team communication skills.
